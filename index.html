Our Story

"Quality in printing is not a skill.
It's a habit — one our family has
practised for 40 years."

Awni Prints carries forward a printing legacy that began in 1985, when our father first stepped into a press and fell in love with the craft. In 1997, our mother joined — and what started as a trade became a family calling.

Decades of hands-on experience taught us something no machine can replicate: the attention to detail, the pride in every print, and the personal care that makes the difference between good work and great work.

Today, under the Awni Prints name, we bring that same generational commitment to quality — with the convenience of WhatsApp ordering, doorstep delivery, and design support that modern businesses deserve.
